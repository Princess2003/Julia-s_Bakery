ABOUT US

Julia'S BAKERY

Julia's Bakery is a wholesale supplier of artisan and craft products, bringing made-to-order bakes fresh to your door.Julia's Bakery started during the covid-19 pandemic where it was discovered by a little girl called Julia and her family.

It is located in a small village called (joppie) in Tzaneen.This bakery was started because we wanted to provide people with bread during the pandemic when they were not able to go out to the shops to buy bread and some other staffs . it was also started to increase employements because most people were losing their jobs due to the pandemic.
